Assistant professor (Akademischer Rat) at [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/go/id/a/?lidx=1) (Germany)
- Affiliated with the [RWTH Chair for AI Methodology](https://www.aim.rwth-aachen.de/) (AIM) and [ADA Research Group](https://ada.liacs.nl/) of Professor Holger H. Hoos
- Principal investigator of [GPBench](https://github.com/GPBench/) and [TinyverseGP](https://github.com/GPBench/TinyverseGP)

## Main research interests

* Genetic Programming
  * Graph representations and variation operators
  * Theoretical and empirical foundations 
* Benchmarking metaheuristics
  * Logic synthesis
  * Policy learning
